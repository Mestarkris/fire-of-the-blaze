◆HOW TO PLAY
Move WASD or arrow keys (drag the on-screen joystick on mobile)
Aim & Shoot point with the mouse, click and hold to fire (auto-aims on mobile - use the shoot button)
Shield Shift key for a brief invincibility window, on a cooldown (or the shield button on mobile)
Mine E or M plants a proximity bomb at your feet (or the mine button on mobile) - one every 10s, up to 6 on the field
Defenders keys 1-4 (or tap their slots) summon a backup fighter for 15s - each has its own weapon, a one-shot power on arrival, and its own long cooldown
Pause Esc or P
Chat your live Blaze chat is the game master - !spawn, !heal, !shield, !slow, !taunt, follows, subs, votes, and tips all affect the run in real time
Vibe the HELP/CHAOS gauge tracks whether chat's been helping or piling on - lean far enough either way and it starts to matter
